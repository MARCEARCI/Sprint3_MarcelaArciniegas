In 1990, a passionate group of ice cream enthusiasts embarked on a delicious journey and founded an ice cream parlor that would become an iconic haven for dessert lovers. Inspired by the idea of offering unique flavors and memorable experiences, they began to experiment with fresh ingredients and boundless creativity.



Discover the freshness and fruity goodness in our delicious Light Fruit Ice Cream. Crafted for health-conscious and flavor-loving individuals, this refreshing treat is the perfect choice for those looking to enjoy a light dessert without compromising on taste.



In our ice cream parlor of the future, the magic of ice cream meets robotic innovation to bring you a unique experience. Our robotic ice cream artisans, with sleek and futuristic designs, are ready to elevate the joy of ice cream to new heights.
